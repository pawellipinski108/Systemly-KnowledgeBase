Funnels in Systemly let you create landing pages and multi-step flows to capture leads and grow your Arbonne business.

## What is a Funnel?

A funnel is a series of web pages designed to guide a visitor toward a specific action — like signing up for a free sample, booking a call, or joining your team.

## Creating a New Funnel

1. Navigate to **Funnels** in the left sidebar
2. Click **+ Add New** to create a new funnel
3. Choose a template or start from scratch
4. Give your funnel a name (e.g., "Free Sample Landing Page")

## Editing Your Pages

Use the drag-and-drop editor to customize your funnel pages:

- Add text, images, and buttons
- Customize colors and fonts to match your brand
- Add forms to collect contact information

## Publishing Your Funnel

When you're happy with your funnel:

1. Click **Save** to save your changes
2. Go to the funnel settings to set your URL path
3. Your funnel will be live at your Systemly domain

## Tips for Success

- Keep your pages simple and focused on one action
- Use clear, compelling headlines
- Always include a call-to-action button
- Test your funnel on mobile devices
