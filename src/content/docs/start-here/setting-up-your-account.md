This guide walks you through the essential first steps after logging into Systemly for the first time.

## Step 1: Complete Your Profile

After logging in, head to **Settings** to fill in your basic information:

- Your name and contact details
- Your business name
- Your timezone

## Step 2: Connect Your Domain (Optional)

If you have a custom domain, you can connect it to Systemly for a professional look on your funnels and landing pages.

## Step 3: Review Your Dashboard

Take a moment to explore the main areas of your Systemly dashboard:

- **Dashboard** — Your overview and quick stats
- **Contacts** — Where all your leads and customers live
- **Conversations** — Messages and communication with contacts
- **Calendars** — Your booking and scheduling tools
- **Funnels** — Your landing pages and sales funnels

## What's Next?

Once your account is set up, explore the other articles in the "Start Here" section to learn how to navigate and use the key features of Systemly.
